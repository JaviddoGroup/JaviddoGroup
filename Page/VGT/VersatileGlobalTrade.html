Services And Products

Reduce the time spent on procurement with Versatile Global Trade.

VGT aims to aid businesses around the world in supplying high amounts of appliances and goods. This includes customizable, tradable, raw, and secondary items. Supplied from leading-quality manufacturers and vendors, Javiddo Group cuts down a significant amount of time that would be spent on procurement, quality control, logistics, and other processes, if handled by the customer itself. Take a look at the carousel below to see the industries from which we supply products.

  • "Electronics (electrical appliances)"

    Providing businesses with essential electrical appliances such as refrigerators, microwaves, and washing machines, ensuring operational efficiency and enhancing employee convenience.

  • Electrical

    Supplying electrical components like cables, switches, and circuit breakers that are crucial for the infrastructure of various business operations, ensuring safety and functionality.

  • Construction materials

    Offering a range of materials such as cement, steel, and insulation to support construction projects, enabling businesses to build and maintain structures effectively.

  • Heavy-duty vehicles

    Providing robust vehicles like trucks and construction machinery that are essential for industries such as logistics and construction, ensuring the efficient transport and handling of heavy loads.

  • Motorcycles

    Supplying motorcycles for delivery services, fleet management, and transportation solutions, helping businesses enhance mobility and operational speed.

  • Agrochemicals

    Offering fertilizers, pesticides, and herbicides to agricultural businesses, promoting healthier crops and higher yields to support the food supply chain.

  • Raw materials and equipment for manufacturing

    Providing essential raw materials and machinery that enable manufacturers to produce goods efficiently, ensuring high-quality output and productivity.

  • Raw materials for general use

    Supplying versatile raw materials used across various industries, supporting diverse business needs, and fostering innovation in product development.

  • Workbenches

    Offering durable workbenches that are vital for workshops and factories, ensuring a stable and efficient workspace for various tasks.

  • Medical supplies and equipment

    Providing essential medical products like syringes, gloves, and diagnostic machines, supporting healthcare facilities in delivering quality care.

  • Hospitality supplies

    Supplying hotels and restaurants with essential items such as linens, toiletries, and kitchen equipment, ensuring guest satisfaction and operational efficiency.

  • Food industry

    Offering a range of products including raw ingredients, packaging materials, and processing equipment to food manufacturers, ensuring the production of safe and high-quality food products.

  • Restaurant supplies

    Providing restaurants with essential supplies such as cooking utensils, commercial ovens, and refrigeration units, supporting efficient kitchen operations.

  • Tableware

    Supplying businesses with high-quality tableware like plates, glasses, and cutlery, enhancing the dining experience for customers in hospitality and food service industries.

  • Textile

    Offering fabrics, threads, and other textile materials to businesses in the fashion and home décor industries, supporting the creation of high-quality products.

  • Advertising supplies and equipment

    Providing materials and tools such as banners, printing machines, and display stands, enabling businesses to effectively promote their products and services.

  • Office supplies

    Supplying essential items like paper, pens, and filing systems to keep business operations smooth and organized.

  • Toys

    Providing a range of toys for retailers, ensuring a diverse selection for customers, and enhancing sales opportunities.

  • Decorations

    Supplying businesses with decorative items such as lighting, art pieces, and seasonal décor to enhance their environment and attract customers.

  • Decorative supplies

    Offering materials like paints, wallpapers, and accessories that businesses can use to create appealing and customized spaces.

  • Perfumery

    Providing raw materials and packaging for the production of perfumes, supporting businesses in creating distinctive fragrance products.

  • Beauty and personal care products

    Supplying a variety of products such as skincare items, cosmetics, and hygiene products to retailers and salons, meeting the growing demand for personal care.

  • Salon supplies

    Offering equipment and products like hair dryers, styling chairs, and shampoos that are essential for the operation of beauty salons.

  • Gym equipment

    Supplying fitness centers with essential equipment such as treadmills, weights, and exercise machines, supporting health and wellness businesses.

  • Cleaning agents

    Providing effective cleaning products for various industries, ensuring hygienic and safe environments for businesses and their clients.

  • Jewelry

    Supplying high-quality materials and finished products to jewelry retailers, enhancing their inventory and customer satisfaction.

  • Clothing

    Offering a range of apparel and accessories to retailers, ensuring a diverse selection for consumers, and supporting fashion businesses in meeting market trends.
