Javiddo Group Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 12.11.2024

Javiddo Group ("we" or "the company") is committed to transparency regarding the use of cookies and similar technologies on our website. This Cookie Policy explains the types of cookies we use, their purposes, and how you can manage your cookie preferences. We respect your privacy and strive to give you control over your experience on our site.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help improve site functionality, remember your preferences, and enhance content relevance.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for website functionality, these cookies do not contain personal information and are not used for tracking.
  • Functionality Cookies: These remember your language, region, and settings, creating a personalized experience.
  • Analytical Cookies: Used for analyzing user behavior through third-party tools like Google Analytics to improve website structure and content.
  • Advertising Cookies: Allow us and our partners to show relevant ads, tracking ad effectiveness on and off the website.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use cookies from third-party services such as Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel to analyze interactions and display personalized ads. Each service provider has its own cookie policies.

4. Cookie Duration

  • Session Cookies: Active only during a single session and deleted once you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for set durations, depending on their purpose.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through:

  • Our Website Settings: Adjust preferences upon your first visit.
  • Browser Settings: Control cookie storage via browser options.
  • Third-Party Tools: Some providers, like Google, offer cookie management tools.

6. Your Privacy and Data Protection

We comply with GDPR and other regulations to protect your personal data.

7. Cookie Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in technology or legislation. We recommend checking this page regularly.

8. Contact Us

For questions, contact us at support@javiddo.com.
